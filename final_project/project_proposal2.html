Purpose

The purpose of the project will be to inform those who are interested in the Rainbow Six Siege professional
circuit.

Target Audience

The target audience for the page will be mainly young men between the ages of 18 and 24.

Page Content

Wireframes

The design of the website will allow it to scale down to fit smaller screens. If the user is on a mobile page,
even though it's not an expected occurance since the website is meant to serve users of a video game that
is most dominant on the PC platform, the ads and whitespace on either side of the content will disappear
giving more space for just the main content. The contents will be kept dynamic so they will shrink to
better fit the screen. No horizontal scrolling here!

Style Guide

Colors

The colors will be kept simple to make the page easiest to read. A simple black or dark blue on white or
dark gray.

Font

For the headers or the main titles, I think Roboto would look nice for the headers for the nav bar, I think Raleway
would look clean and professional. Open Sans would work well for everything else I think.
